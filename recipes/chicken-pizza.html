BBQ Chicken Pizza

Description

A weight loss focused recipe that lets you enjoy the feeling of devouring an entire pizza without suffering from post-feed guilt.

  • 775 calories
  • 76g protein
  • 19g fats
  • 58g carbs

Ingredients

  • 100g High Protein Low Carb Pizza
  • 100g Hi Protein Cottage Cheese
  • 25g Bega Cheese So Light Grated
  • 100g Red Onion
    • 100g Zucchini
  • 90g Leggos Passata Sauce Italian Herbs
  • 30ml MasterFoods BBQ Sauce Reduced S&S
  • 150g Coles Pre-cooked Shredded Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Chop red onions, zucchini and chicken breast separately and into smaller pieces.
  2. Spread a generous amount of BBQ and pizza sauce over pizza base until surface is covered. Do not spread sauce over edges.
  3. Place chicken, zucchini, red onion, cottage cheese and grated cheese over pizza until surface is covered.
  4. Bake pizza in oven at 200 degrees celcius for 10 minutes or until cheese has melted.
