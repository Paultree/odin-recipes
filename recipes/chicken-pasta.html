Chicken Pasta

Description

Healthy, filling and perfect for weight loss goals.

  • 612 calories
  • 62g protein
  • 15g fats
  • 58g carbs

Ingredients

  • 125g Chicken Breast Mince
  • 100g Mutti Sugo Pasta Sauce
  • 25g Coles Mozarella Light Cheese
  • 100g Vetta Smart Protein Pasta
  • Sriracha Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil 500g of water and place pasta in. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until al dente.
  2. Sear mince and break pieces apart until cooked through.
  3. Combine pasta, chicken mince, pasta sauce and cheese together and mix.
  4. Enjoy on its own or with hot sauce.
