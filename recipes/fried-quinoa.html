Fried Quinoa

Description

Macro friendly, low calories, fast and easy to make but is delicious.

  • 365 calories
  • 40g protein
  • 5g fats
  • 36g carbs

Ingredients

  • 75g Snap Frozen Peas
  • 50g Raw Quinoa
  • 63g Egg Whites
  • 100g Chicken Brast Mince
  • Sriracha Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil 500g of water and pour quinoa in. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until all water is gone.
  2. Place quinoa in fridge and let cool for 8 hours or overnight.
  3. Sear mince and break pieces apart until cooked through.
  4. Fry egg whites and break into little pieces.
  5. Steam snap peas until hot.
  6. Combine ingredients in a hot pot and mix until fully combined.
  7. Eat on its own or with hot sauce.
